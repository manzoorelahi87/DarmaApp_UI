Darma - DUBAI AIRPORT RETURNEES MALAYALEE ASSOCIATION

Association was formed during the year 2012 by the members from Kerala worked at Dubai Airports and its associated companies such as Airlines, Civil Aviation, Catering, Dnata, Aviation fuelling, Cargo and any other concerns closely related to Dubai Airport Operations. The membership to the association is restricted for the candidates retired, returned or presently working with Dubai Airports and its associated companies. Association functions in four units such as Thiruvananthapuram, Kochi, Trissur and Calicut under the Central Board.
